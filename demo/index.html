Features

  • User Registration

    User Registration in quizKTC allows students to create accounts, providing access to personalized quizzes, progress tracking, and tailored learning experiences.

  • Category-Based Quizzes

    Category-Based Quizzes in quizKTC organize quizzes by subject or topic, enabling students to focus on specific areas of study for targeted learning.

  • Difficulty Levels

    Difficulty Levels in quizKTC classify quizzes as easy, medium, or hard to match student skill levels and offer progressive challenges.

  • Timed Quizzes

    Implement timed quizzes on quizKTC with countdown timers, auto-submission, and instant feedback to enhance student engagement and assessment accuracy.

  • Leaderboards

    quizKTC display the top-performing students, ranking them based on their quiz scores, completion times, and overall progress, fostering healthy competition and motivation among participants.

  • Instant Feedback

    Instant Feedback in quizKTC provides real-time responses to students' answers, helping them immediately understand their performance and improve learning.

  • Progress Tracking

    quizKTC allows students to monitor their performance over time, providing insights into completed quizzes, scores, areas of improvement, and progress toward their learning goals.
